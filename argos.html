Argos
Design

Argos Design is a boutique design studio that creates hand crafted custom commercial furniture clients across the U.S.

The Challenge

The Argos Design studio founder, Cathy Dipietro, wanted a modern, sleek yet sensible website. She had previously worked with another agency to produce a logo, but wanted to overhaul her website and develop a brand identity across digital channels. The site needed to appeal to business owners, executives and office managers. As well as individuals interested in curating their home offices. With this in mind I decided to explore some furniture design blogs, trends, and websites to better understand the industry.

Role:

Web and UI Design

Deliverables:

Visual Style Guide, Brand Strategy, Visual Mockup, and Web Development (on-going)

The Process

I put together a design brief and worked with Cathy to iron out the details and requirements she deemed most important. Her main objective was making sure a user could easily contact her while navigating the site. I also had to incorporate the previously designed logo into the design, but Cathy was open to making adjustments and gave me free rein over the design direction.

From there we explored visual styles through a mood board which allowed me to hone in on her desired aesthetic. After some feedback on initial wireframes and visual mockups, we agreed on a final design for her site. I then went on to create a visual style guide for her to use on other digital channels in order to present a unified brand.
