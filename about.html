Hi there and welcome! My name is Andres and I’m a visual designer with eight years of experience making digital products and services. I’ve a background working with organizations in the communication, media, and software development fields, from scrappy startups, non-profits, to established large companies. Nowadays I’m passionate about exploring visual styles and am a budding UI animator, web and brand designer. I’m committed to connecting my clients with their desired aesthetic and develop their brand.

Interests

I have strong passion for the arts but I also care about education, altruism, economic empowerment and ecological stewardship. I get excited about projects that allow me to solve design problems while maintaining a focus on ethics, diversity and inclusion. I’m always teaching myself any skill necessary to bring ideas to fruition and I’m not afraid to explore and fail. I’ve been creating content in some shape or form since grade school but recently reignited my passion for digital design. I also have a love for dance and have performed around the Bay Area. You can find me grooving and creating choreographies to cuban salsa, dancehall, urban/funk or afro beats :)
