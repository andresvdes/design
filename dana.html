ABOUT

Dana
Rubin

Dana Rubin is an independent Bay Area illustrator. Her style can be described as joyful, whimsical, and free-form - inspired by nature and human expression. All artwork proceeds go to various nonprofits and charities.

The Brief:

Dana approached me about creating a website for her illustrations that would easily connect art enthusiasts to her online shop and purchase her works. She also wanted to create an online presence starting with her site and to better define her brand.  I was very impressed by her work and jumped on the project right away.

Role:

Web and UI Design

Deliverables:

Visual identity system, UI elements, Visual Mockup, Web Development (on-going)

The Challlenge and Process:

Initially Dana had a vague sense of what her brand should be, but she definitely wanted to channel the same feel her illustrations inspired. I went through and looked at various illustrator’s sites looking at everything from layout, typography and color to learn what it takes to craft a unique voice. With those ideas in mind, I sketched some layout options and presented Dana two wireframes.  

My research allowed me to discover new font types, weights, and subtle layout details that contributed to crafting a custom look. I wanted to dig deeper into what inspired Dana’s illustrations and her visual aesthetic, so I asked her to put together a mood board of visuals that that best represented her desired feel.

Her illustrations served as inspiration for me to create custom navigation elements such as hover icons. They also played nicely with her pair of fonts which added to the whimsical feel of her illustrations.

It all came together well in the final visual mockup of her site. The grid system was a best fit because I wanted users to quickly and easily sift through multiple art works in one go. This layout also elevated her work and did not distract or compete with it visually. Now I’m identifying a platform that will easily allow her to maintain and update content with ease going forward.
