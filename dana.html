ABOUT

Dana
Rubin

Dana Rubin is an independent Bay Area illustrator. Her style can be described as joyful, whimsical, and free-form - inspired by nature and human expression. All artwork proceeds go to various nonprofits and charities.

Dana approached me about creating a gallery hub her illustrations to be easily discovered by art enthusiasts and fellow artists. She wanted the site to present her illustrations with large images, allow users to easily contact her about custom orders, and she wanted her site to seamlessly connect to her e-commerce properties too.

Role:

Web and UI Design

Deliverables:

Visual identity system, UI elements, Visual Mockup, Web Development (on-going)

The challlenge and process

We brainstormed some ideas and then I put together a design brief to capture her target audience, user personas, content and functionality requirements, and a basic sitemap. From there we put together a moodboard to capture her desired colors, aesthetic and visual feel. I followed up with two distinct wireframes and after some review we landed on a simple and clean grid design.
